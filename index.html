Our Story

Founded in 2010, Bean & Brew started with a simple mission: to bring the world's finest coffee beans to our local community. We source directly from sustainable farms and roast every batch in-house.

Whether you need a morning pick-me-up or a quiet place to work, we've got the perfect cup waiting for you.
